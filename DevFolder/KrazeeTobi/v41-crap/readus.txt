This is the sequel to Sonic Doom. EggMan has planned one of his usual
take-over-mobius domination thing, and this time he has decided to make
robot duplicates of EVERYBODY, and giving his robots special powers by
placing a chaos emerald in them! It's up to Sonic, Tails, Knuckles, and
MechaSonic to stop him!

You can pick between Sonic, Tails, MechaSonic and Knuckles to play by
running the appropriate BAT file. Here is a summary of each character:

Sonic - Runs Fastest. Can shoot weapons REALLY fast, but can't jump worth
	beans.

Tails - Runs Slowest. He starts out with 200% health and will be able to
	fly in the future, enabling you to find secrets! 

Knuckles - Runs Medium. He can punch *REALLY* fast, taking an Anime SWATbot
	   down in a VERY short time!

MechaSonic - Runs faster than Knuckles, but slower than Sonic. Has all
	     sorts of cool new weapons. Since he was on Eggman's side once,
	     he can use nearly all the weapons the enemy 'bots can.
